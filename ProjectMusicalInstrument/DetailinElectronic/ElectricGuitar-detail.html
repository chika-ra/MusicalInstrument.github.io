Electric Guitar

The electric guitar is a powerful and versatile instrument used in rock, metal, pop, jazz, and more. It uses electronic pickups to convert string vibrations into sound.

Key Features:

  • Solid or semi-hollow body construction
  • Magnetic pickups for amplified sound
  • Used with amplifiers and effects pedals
  • Popular in rock, blues, and pop genres

Famous Players:

  • Jimi Hendrix
  • Eric Clapton
  • Jimmy Page
  • Slash
