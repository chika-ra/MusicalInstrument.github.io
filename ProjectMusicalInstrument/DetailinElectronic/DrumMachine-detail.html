Drum Machine

A drum machine is an electronic musical instrument that creates percussion sounds, drum beats, and patterns. It is commonly used in electronic, hip hop, and pop music production.

Key Features:

  • Programmed beats and loops
  • Variety of drum kits and effects
  • Used in studio and live performances
  • Syncs with MIDI and DAWs

Popular Models:

  • Roland TR-808
  • Alesis SR-16
  • Elektron Analog Rytm
  • Native Instruments Maschine
