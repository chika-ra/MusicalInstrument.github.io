Sampler

A sampler is an electronic instrument that allows users to record, manipulate, and play back audio samples. It is a core tool in hip-hop, electronic, and experimental music production.

Key Features:

  • Loads and plays back audio clips (samples)
  • Includes pitch shifting, looping, and slicing
  • Used for beat-making, remixing, and live performance
  • Can be hardware-based or software-based

Famous Sampler Users:

  • J Dilla
  • Flying Lotus
  • Daft Punk
