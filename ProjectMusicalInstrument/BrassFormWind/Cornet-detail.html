Cornet

The cornet is a brass instrument similar to the trumpet but with a more compact shape and mellower tone. It is commonly used in brass bands, concert bands, and jazz ensembles. Its conical bore gives it a warmer, more rounded sound compared to the trumpet.

Key Features:

  • Shorter and more compact than a trumpet
  • Conical bore for a mellow tone
