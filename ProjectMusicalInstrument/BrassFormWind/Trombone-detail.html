Trombone

The trombone is a brass instrument characterized by its telescoping slide mechanism that varies the length of the tube to change pitches. Its sound is bold and rich, often used in orchestras, jazz bands, and marching bands.

Key Features:

  • Uses a slide instead of valves
  • Bold, brassy tone suitable for a wide dynamic range
  • Made of brass with a large bell
  • Popular in classical, jazz, and marching band settings

Famous Trombone Players:

  • J.J. Johnson
  • Christian Lindberg
  • Tommy Dorsey
