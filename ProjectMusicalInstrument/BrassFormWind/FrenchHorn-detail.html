French Horn

The French horn is a brass instrument with a long, coiled tubing and a flared bell. It is known for its warm, rich tone and wide range. The French horn is a staple in orchestras and is used in both classical and film music.

Key Features:

  • Coiled brass tubing with a large bell
  • Played with the right hand inside the bell
  • Uses rotary valves for pitch control
  • Wide range and expressive tone
