Accordion

The accordion is a portable, free-reed musical instrument that uses a bellows to force air through reeds. It is widely used in folk, classical, and popular music around the world.

Key Features:

  • Combines keyboard and buttons
  • Produces sound by air moving through metal reeds
  • Used in traditional and folk music
  • Played with both hands: one for melody and one for bass/chords

Famous Accordion Players:

  • Astor Piazzolla
  • Yann Tiersen
  • Myron Floren
