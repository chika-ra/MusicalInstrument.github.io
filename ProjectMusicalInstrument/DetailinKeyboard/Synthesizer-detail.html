Synthesizer

A synthesizer is an electronic instrument that generates audio signals to create a wide range of sounds. It is a staple in electronic music, pop, film scoring, and experimental sound design.

Key Features:

  • Generates sounds using oscillators and filters
  • Supports various synthesis methods (subtractive, FM, wavetable)
  • Highly customizable with knobs, sliders, and modulators
  • Can be analog, digital, or hybrid

Famous Synthesizer Users:

  • Jean-Michel Jarre
  • Vangelis
  • Trent Reznor
