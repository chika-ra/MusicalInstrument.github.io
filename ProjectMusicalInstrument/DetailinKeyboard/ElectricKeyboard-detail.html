Electric Keyboard

An electric keyboard is a versatile electronic musical instrument that produces a variety of sounds, from pianos to synthesizers and drums. It's widely used in education, performance, and music production.

Key Features:

  • Multiple instrument voices and effects
  • Built-in speakers and headphone output
  • MIDI and USB connectivity for recording
  • Touch-sensitive keys and rhythm accompaniments

Famous Keyboard Artists:

  • Ray Charles
  • Stevie Wonder
  • Herbie Hancock
