Organ

The organ is a powerful keyboard instrument known for its majestic and sustained sound. It is commonly used in churches, classical music, and even rock music.

Key Features:

  • Uses air or electronics to produce sound
  • Multiple keyboards (manuals) and pedalboard
  • Massive range and volume capability
  • Commonly found in churches and concert halls
