The banjo is a string instrument with a distinct twangy sound and a circular body. It's mostly used in bluegrass, folk, and country music.

Key Facts:

  • Typically has 4 or 5 strings
  • Known for its bright, lively tone
  • Played with fingers or picks
  • Popular in American folk traditions
