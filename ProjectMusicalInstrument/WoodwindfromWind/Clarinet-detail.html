Clarinet

The clarinet is a woodwind instrument with a single-reed mouthpiece, a straight cylindrical tube, and a flared bell. It is known for its versatile sound, capable of producing warm low notes and bright, clear high notes.

Key Features:

  • Uses a single reed and a cylindrical bore
  • Wide range of dynamics and tone colors
  • Commonly made from wood or plastic
  • Used in classical, jazz, and band music
