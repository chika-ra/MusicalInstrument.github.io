Cymbals

Cymbals are a type of untuned percussion instrument made of thin, normally round plates of various alloys. They are commonly used in orchestras, marching bands, and drum kits to produce crashing or shimmering sounds.

Key Features:

  • Made from bronze or brass alloy
  • Played by clashing together or striking with sticks
  • Produces bright, metallic sound
  • Available in many types: crash, ride, hi-hat, splash, china

Famous Cymbal Users:

  • Neil Peart (Rush)
  • Dave Weckl
  • Travis Barker (Blink-182)
