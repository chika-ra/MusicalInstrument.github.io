Snare Drum

The snare drum is a key component of the drum set known for its sharp, crisp sound. It has wires (snares) stretched across the bottom head to produce a distinctive snap.

Key Features:

  • Has snares for a sharp sound
  • Used for rhythm and accents
  • Essential in both marching and concert bands
