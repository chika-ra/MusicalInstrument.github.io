Triangle

The triangle is a small, triangular metal instrument that produces a bright, tinkling sound. It's struck with a metal beater and is used for rhythmic accents in various music styles.

Key Features:

  • Made of metal and shaped like a triangle
  • Produces a clear, ringing tone
  • Played by striking with a metal beater
