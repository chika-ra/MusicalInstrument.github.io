Bongos

Bongos are a pair of small, open-bottomed hand drums of different sizes. They are played with the fingers and palms and are common in Latin music such as salsa and son cubano.

Key Features:

  • Consist of two drums: macho (small) and hembra (large)
  • Played by hand using various techniques
  • Portable and lightweight
  • Produces sharp, high-pitched tones
