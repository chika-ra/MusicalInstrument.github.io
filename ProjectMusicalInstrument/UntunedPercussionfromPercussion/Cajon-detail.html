Cajon

The cajon is a box-shaped percussion instrument originally from Peru. The player sits on top and strikes the front surface (tapa) with their hands, producing deep bass and sharp snare-like tones.

Key Features:

  • Box drum played by hand
  • Produces both bass and snare tones
  • Used in flamenco, Afro-Peruvian, and acoustic performances
  • Portable and versatile
