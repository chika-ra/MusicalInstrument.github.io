Bass Drum

The bass drum is a large percussion instrument that produces a deep, low sound. It is commonly used in orchestras, marching bands, and drum kits.

Key Features:

  • Produces deep, resonant sound
  • Played with mallets or pedals
  • Found in orchestras and drum sets
