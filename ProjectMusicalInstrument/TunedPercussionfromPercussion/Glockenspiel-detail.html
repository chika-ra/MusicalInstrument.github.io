Glockenspiel

The glockenspiel is a tuned percussion instrument made of metal bars arranged in a keyboard layout. It produces a bright, bell-like sound and is often used in orchestral and marching band music.

Key Features:

  • Metal bars arranged like a keyboard
  • Played with hard mallets
  • Very bright and high-pitched sound
  • Common in classical and cinematic scores

Used By:

  • Orchestras
  • Marching bands
  • Film composers
